Hamburger Recipe

  1. Get ground chuck and make small round balls with the meat. Flatten the meat into the desired size. Remember, the meat may shrink a bit when cooked.
  2. Season the meat with desired seasonings, like garlic salt. Heat up pan and cook until the edges start to gray before flipping.
  3. Keep flipping until both sides are the desired color. I like mine well done, but most people don't.
